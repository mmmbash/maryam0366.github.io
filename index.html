Ademola Adesokan

PhD Student, Computer Science, MIssouri S&T

I am currently pursuing a PhD at the Department of Computer Science within Missouri University of Science and Technology, under the guidance of Dr. Sanjay Madria in the Web to Cloud Computing Lab. My research revolves around utilizing deep learning and natural language processing techniques to address disaster management challenges via social media data. My work primarily concentrates on addressing challenges related to disaster datasets, including decomposing high-level labels into more detailed and descriptive low-level classes, detecting emotions within textual data, and most importantly, building a resilient classification system, among other focus areas in disaster settings.

Before embarking on my PhD journey, I earned a Master of Science degree from the Department of Computer Science at American University of Beirut, where I was supervised by Dr. Shady Elbassuoni. During my master's, I delved into research involving deep learning, information retrieval, and natural language processing, particularly in the fact-checking realm. My thesis primarily tackled challenges of textual claim verification and evidence retrieval from trusted sources.
